Teck is Canada's largest diversified resource company. It operates different mines and metal processing plants in Canada and overseas. Beside the production plants, Teck also has a few research centers which provide technical support to Teck's operation as well as other clients. One of these research centers is called CESL and it is located at Richmond, BC.

CESL is a samll plant with the mission of testing and developing new hydrometallurgical technologies. Hydrometallurgical tests in bench, pilot plant, and demo plant scales can be operated in CESL. After my graduation from UBC in 2015, I worked in this center as process operator for 8 months. I was in charge of operating demo scale leaching autoclave and filter press.
